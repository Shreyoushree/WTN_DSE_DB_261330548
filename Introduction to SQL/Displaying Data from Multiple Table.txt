6. The HR department needs a report on job grades and salaries. To familiarize yourself with the SALGRADE table, first show the structure of the SALGRADE table. Then create a query that displays the name, job, department name, salary, and grade for all employees.	Non-Equi Join	

DESC SALGRADE;
SELECT e.ename,e.job,d.dname,e.sal,s.grade FROM emp e JOIN dept d ON e.deptno=d.deptno JOIN salgrade s ON e.sal BETWEEN s.losal AND s.hisal;
